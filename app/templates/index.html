Service to humanity is service to God. Healing Hands is dedicated to supporting individuals affected by lung cancer by empowering caregivers to provide compassionate, dignified, and excellent care. HOW WE HELP

Supporting Early Detection


Supporting Early Detection helps fund research and screenings to catch lung cancer early, increasing survival rates for all ages.




Advancing New Treatments


Advancing Research for New Treatments funds innovative therapies to improve outcomes and offer hope to lung cancer patients.



Accelerating Clinical Trials


Accelerating Clinical Trials for Lung Cancer speeds up research and treatment breakthroughs, bringing hope to patients and improving outcomes.



Healing Hands provides care for lung cancer patients by empowering their caregivers with compassion, integrity, and excellence. Our mission is to support individuals impacted by lung cancer, ensuring they receive the care and support needed to reach their fullest potential and live with hope and dignity.
